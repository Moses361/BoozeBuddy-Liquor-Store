Tequila

Tequila is a distilled spirit made from the fermented juice of the blue agave plant. Hailing from Mexico, Tequila is known for its bold and distinctive flavors, often featuring notes of agave, citrus, and pepper. Whether sipped neat, used in cocktails like Margaritas, or enjoyed with a slice of lime and salt, Tequila is a versatile and iconic spirit that captures the essence of Mexican culture.
