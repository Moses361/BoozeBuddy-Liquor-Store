Whiskey

Whiskey is a distilled alcoholic beverage made from fermented grain mash. It is aged in wooden barrels, which imparts a range of flavors, including caramel, vanilla, and oak. Whiskey comes in various styles such as Scotch, Bourbon, and Rye, each with its own unique characteristics. Whether enjoyed on the rocks or in classic cocktails, whiskey is celebrated for its depth and complexity.
